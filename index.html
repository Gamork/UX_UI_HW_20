    Atlanta Career Rise USDA Redesign

    • My Pet PAL

    For this group porject we were challanged to create an app from scratch. We decided an app to help pet owners by digitally sharing, tracking, and assigning, the daily task and responsibilities of pet ownership with other caretakers. .

    Problem Statment

    Maintaining a healthy balance for pet owners can be a constant struggle. Family demands, career requirements, and busy social lives can keep us from a regular schedule. Taking the time for pet care is well worth the effort. Balancing care for our companions can increase our opportunities to get out and exercise to keep the whole household healthy. Keeping the family uptodate on pet responsibilities can be a challenge with our busy lives. My Pet Pal will help with this coordination of tasks by keeping everyone in the loop with notifications of their assigned responsibilities. Keeping your pets perky and poised for play time.

    RESEARCH

    The research interviews were conducted with the help of Zoom software. A group effort was put into creating a list of questions for the interviews. The focus on different aspects of pet ownership to allow the interviewees to open up with a mixture of questions concerning daily pet requirements. Play, feeding, restroom, and other responsibilities were the focus of these questions. A trend all of the interviewees mentioned was being on a schedule. Also having to keep track of the schedule while sharing it with others. To assist pet owners with scheduling several products exist already. Physical boards with manual switches lettered with done/ not done is an option that got high reviews websites but did not assist in the daily tracking of pets when you are away or for keeping younger family members accountable while away. Another option is an app. It was able to allow the user to put in a daily reminder notification for tasks. It was only able to be used on one phone, it was not shareable, and did not have a way to know if the task was completed with a tracking graph.
    Our user is a college student who lives in a community living with other students. He has a pet that is beloved by the other residents and is willing to take some of the responsibilities while the owner is at class or other collegiate functions. The demanding schedule has put strain on making sure the pet is well taken care of.

    User Flow and Storyboard

    While designing My Pet Pal an early choice was made to make sure there is a way for people to share and assign reponbilies to each other to make sure there is adequate coverage of care for the users pets. This was confirmed while doing the Feature Prioritization Matrix. Our user interviews informed us that pet owners want to keep track of tasks to complete, when to complete them, whether they were completed, and who will finish them. For this reason, our users will need to add tasks to a calendar, view them, assign them, and mark them complete. The user flow has options to assign a user to a task, add info about the task, and after having created a task, the user can view the task in a list.
